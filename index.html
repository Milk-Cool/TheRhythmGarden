How to play

Thanks for taking your time to test out my game! This is the tutorial for it.

There are green lines representing vines. You'll have to press keyboard keys at the intersections of the vines.

You'll have to press certain keys depending on the letter of the intersection:

L (left) = Z

M (left) = X

R (left) = C

The letters are also colorcoded to improve sightreadability.

There are approach and click circles to help you press at the right time, just like in osu!

You should press the corresponsing key when the approach circle shrinks to the same size as the click circle:

And that's basically it! Good luck :D

Mobile devices aren't supported (yet).
